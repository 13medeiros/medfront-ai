# Chroma

## Purpose

Create and validate a color system that is harmonious, accessible, semantically clear and coherent with the product identity.

## Inputs

Read PRODUCT.md, IDENTITY.md and QUALITY.md before starting.

## Process

1. Identify emotional and functional color requirements.
2. When no palette exists, propose three distinct directions.
3. Explain harmony, temperature, saturation and intended perception.
4. Select one direction with clear reasoning.
5. Define tokens for canvas, surfaces, text, borders, brand, accent, focus and semantic states.
6. Keep brand, success, availability, selection and focus distinguishable.
7. Define approximate distribution of neutral, brand, accent and semantic colors.
8. Test representative screens, controls, charts and states.
9. Validate contrast and never communicate state by color alone.
10. Update IDENTITY.md with rationale, tokens and combinations to avoid.

## Color Score

Harmony: 20
Contrast and readability: 20
Semantic roles: 15
Distribution and balance: 15
Identity coherence: 15
State accessibility: 10
Light and dark consistency: 5

Minimum certification score: 75 out of 100.

## Output

Report the selected palette, rejected alternatives, token table, contrast issues, Color Score, open findings and validation plan.
