# Quality Standard

## Score areas

Concept and originality: 15

Art direction: 15

Typography and composition: 10

Narrative and hierarchy: 10

Interaction quality: 10

Motion quality: 10

Technical quality: 10

Performance: 5

Responsiveness: 5

Accessibility: 5

Content quality: 5

Total: 100

## Score meaning

0 to 49: functional but generic

50 to 64: solid commercial interface

65 to 74: premium execution

75 to 84: distinctive experience

85 to 92: standout quality

93 to 100: rare execution quality

## Final checks

- No unresolved critical findings.
- Keyboard navigation works.
- Reduced-motion behavior exists.
- Core layouts were visually reviewed at required viewport sizes.
- Empty, loading, error and extreme-content states were tested.
- Essential content remains available without advanced interaction.
- Performance trade-offs are documented.
